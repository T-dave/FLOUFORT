Who we are

We are formidable company that engages in the provision of consultancy services in the areas; Accounting , Auditing, Taxation, Financial & Management consultancy. Our services are high quality professional consultancyservices backed up with a wide spectrum of experienced and skilled professionals and experts.

Book keeping & Accounting

Every business whether big or small needs to keep financial records. By letting us take care of your accounting requirements, you can be rest assured that all is being taken care of professionallyand efficiently.

Taxation

At FLOUFORT, we have tax experts who possess deep understanding of tax laws and also the client’s business. We ensure our individual and corporate clients pay the least possible tax while also complying with domestic and international tax laws.

Audit and Assurance Services

FLOUFORT audit team work with organisations in both the public and private sector to enhanced their corporate reporting and support their compliance with regulatory requirements.

Human Resources

The services cover the whole area of human resource in any organization, both public and private sector, and include identification of appropriate mix of manpower requirement, personnel recruitment, training and development. It covers staff evaluation, appraisal, promotion, remuneration and welfare packaging.

Project Management

FLOUFORT Project Management Services are designed to assist clients with a number of large scale projects, allowing you to focus on your core business objectives. Our experienced Project Managers are trained to guide project coordination, communication and project budget management to assure your projects are completed on schedule and within budget, avoiding costly overruns, saving you both time and money.

Financial Advisory Services

Based on our thorough understanding of management accounting systems, we provide financial management advice to clients on operational and financing structures. This includes preparation of financial analysis and business plans, as well as facilitating project financing.
